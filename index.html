About me

Hello! I’m Lucas Acuña, an IT professional from Uruguay passionate about technology and innovation. My journey in IT began with my studies in Computer Engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in programming and systems design. Alongside my academic path, I complemented my knowledge with specialized courses in web development, where I explored HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript, as well as advanced topics like networking and cloud computing. Currently, I’m honing my skills in cloud infrastructure and IT services as a Tier 3 Active Directory Support Engineer at Microsoft. I specialize in directory services, Azure integrations, and Windows Server, solving complex global incidents and delivering tailored technical solutions. I also have extensive experience in Salesforce development, working with tools like Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), and process automation to create impactful solutions. My achievements include earning the Salesforce Platform Developer 1 and Salesforce Associate certifications, alongside practical expertise gained through Trailhead projects and Udemy Enterprise courses. In addition, I have hands-on experience with Java and continue to expand my expertise across technologies. I’m driven to tackle new challenges and create innovative solutions in the IT world.
